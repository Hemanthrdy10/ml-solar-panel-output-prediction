SolarSense: An ML-Powered Solar Panel Output Prediction

To facilitate reliable solar energy production, machine learning plays a crucial role in enhancing reliability through accurate predictions. Users are invited to input their location, date, time, and relevant environmental data. Our Random Forest Regressor model, trained on solar power and weather information, offers precise predictions tailored for each season. Simply click "Predict solar power" to input your data and receive reliable forecasts for solar panel output.

Predict Solar Power

In our SolarSense project, dedicated to solar output prediction, we systematically assessed various machine learning techniques: Decision Tree, Gradient Boosting, Support Vector Machine, and Random Forest.

Following thorough evaluation, Random Forest emerged as the most effective option. This superiority became more pronounced post-seasonal optimization, where it demonstrated significant enhancements, yielding the lowest error rates across all seasons. Its proficiency in managing complex data and mitigating noise and outliers highlights its suitability for our project, affirming Random Forest as the optimal choice for precise solar output prediction.
